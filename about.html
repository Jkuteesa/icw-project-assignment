Know More About Us

EduAll is to be a catalyst for transforming education in Africa, and to see every child given the opportunity to thrive and fulfil their God-given potential. We work towards this vision both by walking alongside school communities and by investing deeply in the lives of others who have the potential to bring change.

This means building and upgrading schools that are child-friendly, disability and gender-sensitive, and provide safe, nonviolent, and inclusive spaces for kids ...

Mission Statement: "Empowering Uganda's future generations through accessible, inclusive, and quality education, bridging the gap for disadvantaged children to reach their full potential."

Vision Statement: "A Uganda where every child, regardless of background, has equal access to quality education, unlocking their potential to become active contributors to society and catalysts for positive change."

Call To Action

"Join us in shaping Uganda's future! Support our mission to provide quality education to disadvantaged children. Donate, volunteer, or partner with us to make a lasting impact. Together, let's unlock the potential of Uganda's next generation!"
